Privacy Policy

Summary: JobNexus is a student job portal built as a project at Centurion University. We take your privacy seriously and do not sell your personal data. This policy explains what we collect and why.

1. Data We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you register an account or use our services:

  • Account Information: Full name, email address, and password (stored as a secure hash via Firebase Authentication).
  • Profile Information: Skills, educational qualifications, work experience, resume PDF, and profile photo (optional).
  • Recruiter Information: Company name, designation, and job postings you create.
  • Application Data: Jobs you apply for and the status of those applications.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, and browser/device type (collected automatically via Firebase Analytics).

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your account and provide platform features.
  • To match students with relevant job and internship opportunities.
  • To allow recruiters to review applications and manage their job postings.
  • To send transactional emails — such as application status updates and account notifications.
  • To improve the platform through aggregated, anonymised analytics.
  • To comply with legal obligations when required.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party.

3. Cookies

JobNexus uses essential cookies and browser local storage to maintain your session state (i.e., to keep you logged in) and store UI preferences such as theme selection. We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels.

You can clear cookies and local storage data at any time via your browser settings. Doing so will log you out of the platform.

4. Third-Party Services

JobNexus is built on the following third-party services, each with their own privacy practices:

  • Google Firebase — Authentication, Firestore database, and file storage. Data is stored in Firebase servers. See Firebase Privacy Policy.
  • Vercel — Hosting and deployment. See Vercel Privacy Policy.
  • EmailJS — Used to send contact form submissions to our team. See EmailJS Privacy Policy.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data:

  • Passwords are never stored in plain text — Firebase Authentication handles credential management securely.
  • Firebase Security Rules restrict database and storage access so users can only read/write their own data.
  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS.

While we take security seriously, no system is completely immune to breaches. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password and to log out from shared devices.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights with respect to your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information directly from your profile settings.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us.
  • Portability: Request an export of your profile data in a machine-readable format.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@jobnexus.in or via our Contact page.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, we will remove your personal data from our systems within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal reasons.

8. Children's Privacy

JobNexus is intended for users aged 16 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16. If we become aware that a child under 16 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of JobNexus after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please reach out:

  • Email: privacy@jobnexus.in
  • Address: Centurion University of Technology and Management, Bhubaneswar, Odisha, India
  • Contact Form: jobnexus.in/pages/contact.html
